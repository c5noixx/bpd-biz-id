Navigation System

Ben-PD integrates navigation systems essential for safe and precise aircraft routing, approach, and landing operations. Our solutions include the harmonization of NAVAIDS such as DVOR/DME, NDB, ILS components, radar systems, ADS-B sensors, and supporting communication links. We ensure optimal signal performance, interference reduction, and redundancy design to maintain reliability under demanding operational conditions. The result is a navigation ecosystem that delivers accurate guidance, airspace efficiency, and compliance with ICAO Annex standards.
